Wearable form coaching

Feel every rep.
Fix every mistake.

Joint trackers with haptics track your movement in real time and vibrate on the joints that are off—so you correct your form as you lift. After your set, get a clear rep-by-rep report and know exactly what to fix next time.

Joint tracking + haptic feedback

How it works

1

Wear the trackers

Straps on wrists, elbows, knees, and spine. Lightweight and discreet, stays out of your way.

2

Real-time haptic feedback

When a joint drifts from ideal form, that tracker vibrates. You feel which part to correct-without looking at a screen.

3

Rep-by-rep report

After your set, see which rep had issues, which joints were off, and simple cues to fix them next time.
